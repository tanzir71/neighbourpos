SYS - 06

What it deliberately does not do

Honest limits keep the tool tiny, auditable, and deployable on ordinary hosting.

No payment processing

NeighbourPOS records payment status only. Pair it with your existing cash, card, or online payment flow.

No vendor messaging baked in

SMS and email are placeholders so each store can choose Twilio, SendGrid, Mailgun, or simple CSV outreach.

No high-scale claims

SQLite is perfect for tiny stores and demos. Use Postgres/MySQL when concurrency and operations outgrow the file.
